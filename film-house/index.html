THE VISION

A New Chapter for Albanian Cinema

Icons Film House represents our ambitious vision for the future of Albanian cinema.
More than just a venue, it will be a cultural hub where the legacy of our icons meets the creativity of new generations.

We envision a space dedicated to screenings of classic Albanian films, workshops with master artists, and the development of new works that carry forward our rich storytelling tradition.

The Roadmap

Our plans for bringing this vision to life

PHASE I

The Archive

Building the most comprehensive digital archive of Albanian cinema history.

PHASE II

The Screenings

Curated film events celebrating classic and contemporary Albanian cinema.

PHASE III

The House

A physical space dedicated to film, education, and cultural exchange.
