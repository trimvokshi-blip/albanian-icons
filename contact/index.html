Get in touch

Albanian Icons is an ongoing cultural project dedicated to preserving and presenting Albanian cinema and cultural memory through a curated, editorial lens.

We welcome conversations related to cultural collaboration, archival material, interviews, research, and editorial contributions.
If you are an artist, filmmaker, researcher, or institution interested in contributing to or collaborating with Albanian Icons, we would be glad to hear from you.

For general inquiries, collaborations, or editorial discussions, please contact us directly or reach out through our social channels.

We believe meaningful cultural dialogue begins with conversation.
